UK Hammer believes that great design is a blend of function and beauty. We work closely with our clients to understand their needs and deliver spaces that reflect their personality and lifestyle.

“Each project tells a story — from worn and outdated to fresh and inspired. See the before-and-after results of UK Hammer team renovation designs...”

Hello! I’m Omid — I grew up in the vibrant city of Tehran before making the exciting move to London and later settling in the creative coastal town of Plymouth. My journey across continents has shaped not just my worldview, but also my passion for craftsmanship.

I hold a Level 3 NVQ in Carpentry and Joinery, alongside certifications in domestic electrical installation and advanced painting techniques. My goal is simple yet meaningful: to transform everyday homes into inspiring personal spaces through smart design and honest labor.

When I'm not renovating homes, you'll find me sketching architecture, hiking through moorlands, binge-watching DIY shows, or enthusiastically fixing things that aren't even broken — because why not?
